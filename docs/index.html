Chapter 1 Supplementary Material for IMiC Intervention Effects Manuscript

This online supplementary appendix provides the complete analytical outputs supporting the manuscript
“The impact of nutritional interventions on human milk in low resource settings: findings from the International Milk Composition (IMiC) Consortium.”

1.1 Organization of the Online Supplement

1.1.1 Study Baseline Characteristics

Detailed maternal, infant, and household characteristics for the IMiC subsamples across trials, including comparisons with parent trial populations and baseline balance across intervention arms.

1.1.2 Human Milk Component Distributions

Unadjusted distributions of all measured human milk components, including macronutrients, micronutrients, B-vitamins, HMOs, targeted metabolites, proteins, and microbiome features, stratified by study and visit where applicable.

1.1.3 Adjusted Intervention Effects on Individual Milk Components

Covariate-adjusted average treatment effect estimates for primary outcomes (macronutrients and micronutrients), with false-discovery rate correction applied by outcome group.

1.1.4 Unadjusted Intervention Effects on Individual Milk Components

Corresponding unadjusted estimates for all milk components.

1.1.5 Unscaled Intervention Effects on Individual Milk Components

Corresponding adjusted, but unscaled estimates for all milk components, using the raw concentration values without Z-scoring for comparison across milk components.

1.1.6 Targeted and Exploratory Milk Components

Results for tertiary and exploratory outcomes, including targeted metabolomics, untargeted metabolomics, untargeted proteomics, and milk microbiome analyses, with pathway- and set-level summaries.

1.1.7 Subgroup analyses stratified by maternal BMI

Effect modification analyses stratified by maternal BMI category (underweight vs not)

1.1.8 Trajectory analyses of human milk component change across lactation

Visualization of longitudinal trajectories of selected milk components across lactation by study and intervention arm.
